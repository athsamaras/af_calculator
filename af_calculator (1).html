⚠ Research Use Only. This tool is intended for research and educational purposes only. It has been developed and validated in a Greek cardiology registry (N=12,324) and should not be used as the sole basis for clinical decisions. All predictions should be interpreted by a qualified clinician in the context of the complete clinical picture. External validation: C-index 0.676 (Cox), tAUC 5yr 0.730.

Model notes: The Cox model prediction is computed exactly using the published coefficients and stratum-specific baseline survival functions (S₀ at 5 years: VHD=0: 0.9140, VHD=1: 0.9237, VHD=2: 0.9030), with E/O recalibration factor=1.318. RSF and XGBoost predictions shown here are SHAP-weighted approximations for illustrative purposes — the full non-linear models require server-side computation and are available via the study GitHub repository. The Cox model is the primary clinical reference.
